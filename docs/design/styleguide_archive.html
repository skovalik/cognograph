Tone & Voice

Character

Technical but approachable. Professional, clear, and concise. Not playful or cute. Not corporate or stiff. Speaks with confidence and precision.

Writing Style

Short sentences. Active voice. Specific language over vague. Lead with what the user can do, not what the product is.

Brand Descriptors

Spatial Intelligent Connected Focused Crafted Precise
Avoid

Generic AI buzzwords ("revolutionary", "next-gen", "powered by AI"). Name-brand comparisons. Overpromising capabilities. Vague superlatives. Emoji in product copy.
