Mission & Vision

Why we exist and where we're going.

Mission
To give spatial thinkers a workspace where visual arrangement becomes functional context — where the way you organize ideas directly shapes how AI understands them.
Vision
A future where complex knowledge work happens on spatial canvases, where AI sees what you see, and where the interface itself is a thinking tool — not just a container for thought.

Core Values

The principles that guide everything we build and say.

1. Spatial Thinking First
We believe arrangement conveys meaning. Visual organization isn't decoration — it's data. The canvas is a workspace, not just a display.
2. Connections Have Consequences
Links between nodes aren't just visual — they're functional. Context flows through connections. Structure shapes understanding.
3. Local-First, User-Owned
Your data stays on your machine by default. You control what syncs and where. No forced cloud, no lock-in.
4. Craft Over Features
Every interaction should feel designed. Ship fewer things, done well. Polish matters.
5. Power Without Complexity
Advanced capability, accessible interface. Progressive disclosure of complexity. Defaults that work, options that scale.

Positioning Statement

How we describe what we are and who we're for.

For spatial thinkers and AI power users who need to orchestrate complex AI workflows, Cognograph is a canvas-based workspace where visual arrangement becomes functional AI context. Unlike linear chat interfaces that lose context and require constant re-explanation, Cognograph lets you see relationships, fork conversations, and have AI understand your spatial organization as meaningful structure.

Cognograph reimagines how you work with AI by making spatial arrangement functional.

Today's AI tools are linear. You start a chat, explain your context, get an answer, and start over. Complex projects require constant re-explanation. Context lives in your head, not the tool.

Cognograph puts AI conversations on a spatial canvas. Create nodes for conversations, projects, notes, tasks, and artifacts. Arrange them visually. Connect them meaningfully. Those connections aren't decoration — they're data.

When you start a conversation with AI, Cognograph automatically includes context from connected nodes. The AI sees the same relationships you see. Your spatial arrangement becomes the AI's understanding.

Key capabilities:

  • Infinite canvas — Arrange ideas spatially, zoom in and out, see the big picture
  • Typed nodes — Conversations, Projects, Notes, Tasks, Artifacts with purpose-built behaviors
  • Context injection — Connections feed automatically into AI prompts
  • Conversation forking — Branch from any point to explore alternatives
  • Local-first — Your data stays on your machine by default

Cognograph is built for spatial thinkers and AI power users who've hit the limits of linear chat. It's for researchers managing complex literature reviews, writers building narrative structures, developers orchestrating AI-assisted coding projects, and anyone who thinks visually.

Do's & Don'ts

Guidelines for maintaining brand consistency.

Logo Usage

DO
  • Use approved logo files
  • Maintain clear space around logo
  • Use on appropriate backgrounds
  • Keep proportions consistent
DON'T
  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change logo colors outside palette
  • Add effects (shadows, glows)
  • Place on busy backgrounds
  • Use smaller than 24px height

Tone & Messaging

DO
  • Be confident but humble
  • Lead with benefits, not features
  • Use specific examples
  • Sound like a human who cares
  • Acknowledge limitations honestly
DON'T
  • Use superlatives (best, revolutionary)
  • Use corporate speak (leverage, synergize)
  • Claim "AI-powered" (everything is now)
  • Be dismissive of alternatives
  • Overpromise features or timeline

Visual Style

DO
  • Use dark mode as primary
  • Use real screenshots
  • Keep animations subtle
  • Maintain visual hierarchy
  • Use purple as accent sparingly
DON'T
  • Use neon or high-contrast "AI aesthetic"
  • Use gradients that look like ChatGPT
  • Use rounded "chat bubble" styling
  • Use stock photography
  • Add excessive animations
